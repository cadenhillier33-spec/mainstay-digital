AI Business Management

What it actually means — in plain English.

You don't need to understand AI to use it. We handle the entire setup, installation, and ongoing management. Here's what happens after we turn it on.

1

We come to you.

We install a dedicated Mac Mini at your office. This is your business's personal AI system — it runs locally, stays on 24/7, and is completely managed by us. You don't touch it.

2

We connect it to your business.

We wire it into the tools you already use — your website, email, phone system, booking software, review platforms. Whatever your customers use to reach you, we connect it.

3

It starts working immediately.

New leads get responded to in under 60 seconds. Common questions get answered automatically. People who don't book get followed up with. And you get a weekly report of everything it handled.
