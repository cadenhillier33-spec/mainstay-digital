AI Business Management

What it actually means — in plain English.

You don't need to understand AI to use it. We handle the entire setup, installation, and ongoing management. Here's what happens after we turn it on.

1

We come to you.

We install a dedicated Mac Mini at your office. This is your business's personal AI system — it runs locally, stays on 24/7, and is completely managed by us. You don't touch it.

2

We connect it to your business.

We wire it into the tools you already use — your website, email, phone system, booking software, review platforms. Whatever your customers use to reach you, we connect it.

3

It starts working immediately.

New leads get responded to in under 60 seconds. Common questions get answered automatically. People who don't book get followed up with. And you get a weekly report of everything it handled.

Real examples — by industry
Restaurant

A customer fills out your catering inquiry form at 11pm. Instead of waiting until tomorrow, they get a professional response in 45 seconds with your catering menu, pricing, and a link to book a tasting — while your staff is home asleep.

Medical Practice

A new patient submits an appointment request. They're immediately sent intake forms, insurance info needed, and directions to your office — no receptionist needed. Patients who cancel get re-engaged with alternative time slots automatically.

Real Estate

Someone clicks "Schedule a Showing" on one of your listings at 9pm. They get a response confirming their time slot, the property address, and your agent's direct line — before a competitor's agent even sees the inquiry.

Law Firm

A potential client fills out your "Free Consultation" form. They immediately receive a professional intake questionnaire and a calendar link to schedule their call — while your paralegal focuses on active cases instead of triaging inbound.
